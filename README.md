# E-Commerce Sales & Customer Analytics

## Business Problem
Olist (Brazilian E-Commerce) CEO wants to grow revenue by 15% in Q4. 
The goal is to identify which customer **segments, regions, and product 
categories** to focus on for maximum revenue growth.

## Key Insights
-  Top 5% customers contribute 23% of total revenue
-  São Paulo = 37% of all revenue (7.7M BRL)
-  Bed & Bath Table = highest revenue category (1.74M BRL)
-  28.9K customers are At-Risk — need immediate retention strategy
-  69.59% payments via Credit Card
